    Site: 12 A, Sector 127, Noida

    Overview
    Easy to navigate and strategically located, the state-of-the-art MMR Corporate Square lies on the super corridor of development. From business spaces to fully equipped ready-to-work offices, this establishment offers flexible options with all amenities, providing a perfect integrated business environment.

    Some of the major highlights of this building are:

    • An advanced business complex spread across 4,200 sq. meters
    • An Indian Green Building Council (IGBC) certified platinum building
    • A G+9 iconic earthquake resistant structure
    • Approx 1.54 lakh sq. ft. (built up area)
    • Floor plate approx 12.000 sq. ft.
    • Abutting 45 meters wide road, two-level basement parking
    • 100 per cent power backup
    • Centrally air-conditioned building
    • Passive and active strategies, optimally designed to minimize energy consumption resulting in savings approximately 40 per cent energy over ASH RAE-based buildings
    • This campus to be a ZERO-discharge campus, the entire rainwater to be collected and carried to the harvesting tanks

    Raghav Global School, Noida

    (Ramkali Bhola Singh Educational Society)

    Raghav Global School is a part of the RBS Educational Society started by Shri Mahipal Singh Raghav in Mandawali in year 2010. The society believes in what Gandhiji said that the real India lived in its villages & real progress for its people could only come through better educational opportunities. The objective of this Society is to create an institution which offers affordable education, covering schools & vocational training centers in a wide range of disciplines such as technology, hospitality, health and management areas. The Society is administered by a team of professional visionaries, and is headed by Commodore P. I. Oommen, VSM, an educationist and administrator with long and varied experience of over 50 years.

    Raghav Global School, Bulandshahr

    (Ramkali Bhola Singh Educational Society)

    Raghav Global School was started in April 2012 with the Playgroup, Nursery, Pre-Primary and Classes I, II & III. The school plans for building 150 spacious rooms, comprising new concept classrooms; laboratories; libraries; activity rooms; auditorium; audio visual halls and a full range of outdoor and indoor games and recreational facilities in the next few years. The school will have the most modern auditorium with a seating capacity of 600 people. The school also plans to have a well equipped medical room for First Aid and minor injuries. A special scheme has been drawn up to reduce the weight of the school bags through innovative methods.
